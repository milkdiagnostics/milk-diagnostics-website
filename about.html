Why We Exist

Milk already contains the data.

Every milking event contains information about cow health, nutrition, reproductive status, and milk quality. Historically, most of that information has remained inaccessible because testing is intermittent, manual, or delayed.

A monthly lab test captures a snapshot. A bulk tank result gives a herd average. Neither provides the individual-cow, milking-by-milking resolution needed to support the decisions dairy operations actually make every day.

Milk Diagnostics was created to make continuous inline measurement operationally practical — to close the gap between what milk contains and what dairy operations can currently see.

What We’re Building

Three capabilities. One continuous measurement platform.

Each capability builds on the same underlying measurement event — the same inline sensing, the same milking routine, the same infrastructure. No additional steps required.

Inline measurement

Continuous measurement integrated directly into milking workflows. Multiple indicators captured from Merlin's inline measurement at every milking event, attributed to the individual cow.

Longitudinal visibility

Structured historical records across milkings, lactation stages, and herd trends. Individual baselines that make deviations legible — not against population averages, but against each cow’s own history.

Operational intelligence

Actionable monitoring for producers, processors, veterinarians, and research teams. The same underlying data, surfaced differently depending on the decision that needs to be made.

Our Approach

Scientific rigor. Operational practicality.

We are not building a sensor product. We are building measurement infrastructure — the kind that dairy operations can depend on at commercial throughput, in commercial environments, without adding steps or changing workflows.

Measurement first

Accuracy is non-negotiable

Deploying inline measurement at commercial throughput requires rigorous validation across diverse dairy environments, temperatures, and flow conditions. That is where our technical effort is concentrated.

Operational by design

No additional steps

The platform integrates into the existing milking workflow. No diversion. No sample handling. No change to parlor routine. Data is generated automatically at every milking event, for every cow, without adding labour requirements to the operation.

Data that compounds

Value increases over time

Individual cow records at milking frequency accumulate into longitudinal datasets that become more useful with every reading. The platform is designed for this — not for one-time diagnostics, but for continuous operational visibility that improves as history builds.

Deliberate rollout

Partnership over scale

Early-stage deployment is intentionally focused. We work closely with a small group of producers, processors, and research partners to validate measurement performance in real environments before pursuing commercial scale.
